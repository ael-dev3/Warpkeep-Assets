## Model correspondence and visual QA

The polished GLB and Blend share all 17 mesh names, both material names, and the same 41,334-triangle topology. The GLB differs intentionally: export seams increase its upload-vertex count, its bounds are normalized for runtime delivery, and it includes six animation clips while the editable Blend retains the corresponding static 47-bone rig without stored actions.

Local-only Blender 5.2 renders show the same black draft horse, wooden slat wagon, purple-and-gold tack and cargo, dual wheels, banner, and lit lantern. The no-telescope payload visibly omits the telescope while retaining all other core parts. No missing textures, clipping, broken geometry, or obvious rig corruption was observed. Those QA renders are not part of the public archive.
